The Political Layer (inside the onion layer)

The dark forest isn’t “outside politics.” It’s where politics gets concentrated.

Privacy as Shield

For marginalized people, encryption and gated spaces can be survival infrastructure.

But the same affordances can be used by harassers, scammers, and states.

Privacy as Veil

Elites can coordinate through “cozy” channels with asymmetric power and low accountability.

The quietest rooms can be the ones that move the loudest policy.

The Asymmetry

When counterpublics move into hidden spaces, they gain cohesion but lose contestation and broad visibility. Hegemonic actors keep the visibility and the private channels.
